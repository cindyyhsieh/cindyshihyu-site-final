Biography

Cindy Shih-Yu Hsieh is a Taiwanese cellist with a deep commitment to both performance and musical collaboration. She earned her Master of Music from the Mannes School of Music in 2024, where she studied under Jay Campbell, and her Bachelor of Arts in Music from Soochow University.

謝仕妤是一位來自台灣的大提琴家，專注於獨奏、重奏與樂團演出，並積極參與各類音樂合作計畫。她於2024年取得紐約曼尼斯音樂學院碩士學位，師承傑出大提琴家 Jay Campbell，並於東吳大學取得音樂學士學位。
